About SpinLogic

SpinLogic is a project aimed towards anyone in the tetr.io community that wants to improve their skill. It analyzes your performance and gives you advice on how to improve. Utilizing a AI model, it gives the best move in a given situation, modeled after your own gameplay. It also provides you with exercises tailored to your weaknesses, bolstering your skilset and improving your game.
